Front-End development solely focuses on client-side development. These developers' job is to work on the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript part of a website. These are not the only languages they mainly focused on, but these are the primary pillars of any website you see today. The Hypertext-Markup language(HTML) and Cascading Stylesheet(CSS) may not have changed much over the years except their new updated versions, but JavaScript has evolved considerably over the years. Even though a developer is working on the Front-End, their job starts developing an idea to present it to the Back-End developers. A developer with significant knowledge about Front-End development can also explore Web-Design, Software Engineer, or a Project Engineer.
